Open source medical analytics in an open cloud.

ChRIS reduces the barrier to cloud and distributed computing for researchers and developers. While geared initially to medical image analytics, ChRIS is a general purpose distributed data and computation platform. Easily convert existing analytics or create new ones and immediately have the benefits of distributed containerized workflows.

ChRIS workflows can run anywhere -- on your laptop, in your lab, on an HPC, or in the cloud.

Through our strong partnership with Red Hat, Inc and the Massachusetts Open Cloud (MOC), you can moreover deploy workflows to the MOC — while still maintaining ownership of your own data.

ChRIS is the platform that makes it possible and easy.
